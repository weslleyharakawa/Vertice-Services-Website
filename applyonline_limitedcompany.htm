Capital / Shareholder
Please, add the initial capital of your company. Example: Capital = £ 100,00 which means the company has 100 shares of £1,00 each.
Total Capital
Number of shares
Value of each share
Please include the shareholders of the company. The shareholders are the owners of the company. Lets say for instance the company has two shareholders ( 50% of ownership for each partner) . Each one has 50% of the company and the total capital is £1.000,00 divided in 100 shares of £10,00 each. This structure will result in each shareholder having 50 shares of £10,00  You can be a director as well as a shareholder in the business which means that you own and work in the business.
